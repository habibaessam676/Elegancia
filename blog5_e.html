The Arabian Golden Age

Alchemists like Avicenna perfected distillation, making alcohol-based perfumes possible and spreading the art across the world.

Modern Perfumery

France became the center in the 18th century, with Grasse still known as the perfume capital. Today, we blend tradition with innovation.
